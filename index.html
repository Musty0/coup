Players
Quick Guide
🏁
Goal Be the last seat with influence remaining.
Each seat starts with 2 influence. When a seat loses influence, it reveals a card. Revealed cards are out.
👥
Teams (Leader / Advisor) The seat plays the turn, not an individual.
In a 2-person seat:
  • Leader performs actions and confirms decisions.
  • Advisor can suggest picks to the Leader (no execution).
  • When the Advisor suggests a target or action, a teal dot appears next to that player's name or action button.
🎯
Targeting Tap a seat to set your target.
Actions like Steal / Assassinate / Coup use your selected target.
🪙
Actions (coins) Core actions + costs in one place.
  • Income: +1 coin (always safe)
  • Foreign Aid: +2 coins (can be blocked by Duke)
  • Coup: pay 7 → target loses 1 influence (cannot be blocked/challenged)
  • 10+ coins: you must Coup
🛡️
Blocks & Counters Who can stop what, at a glance.
Action Blocked by
Foreign Aid Duke
Steal Captain or Ambassador
Assassinate Contessa
Coup Cannot be blocked
Blocks can be challenged. If caught bluffing a block, you lose 1 influence.
🃏
Roles You can claim actions/blocks. Claims can be challenged.
If you’re challenged and you were lying, your seat loses 1 influence.
💰
Duke Tax +3 • Blocks Foreign Aid
  • Action: Tax → gain +3 coins.
  • Block: Stops Foreign Aid.
🗡️
Assassin Assassinate (pay 3)
  • Action: Pay 3 → target loses 1 influence.
  • Blocked by: Contessa.
🏴‍☠️
Captain Steal • Blocks Steal
  • Action: Steal coins from target.
  • Block: Stops someone stealing from you.
  • Also blocked by: Ambassador.
🔄
Ambassador Exchange • Blocks Steal
  • Action: Exchange → choose cards to keep.
  • Block: Stops someone stealing from you.
🛡️
Contessa Blocks Assassinate
  • Block: Stops an assassination targeting you.
  • Can be challenged: If you claimed Contessa and don’t have it, you lose 1 influence.
⚔️
Challenge / Block When to press the red button.
  • Challenge if you think a role claim is a lie.
  • If you challenge and you’re wrong, you lose 1 influence.
  • Blocks stop certain actions (blocks can be challenged too).
